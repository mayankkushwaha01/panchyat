About Blockchain Land Records

Our blockchain-based land record system offers several advantages:

  • Immutability: Once recorded, land records cannot be altered without consensus.
  • Transparency: All transactions are visible to authorized parties.
  • Security: Cryptographic hashing ensures data integrity.
  • Reduced Disputes: Clear ownership records reduce land disputes.
